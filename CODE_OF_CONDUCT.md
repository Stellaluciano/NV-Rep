# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to foster an open, welcoming, and respectful environment for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness.
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback.
- Prioritizing scientific rigor and collaborative integrity.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, or disruptive conduct.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces and in public spaces when representing the project.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers.
